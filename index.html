About Burbank AI

Founded in 2022 by a team of AI engineers, data scientists, and sales professionals, Burbank AI was born from a simple observation: local businesses were hemorrhaging money on ineffective advertising while struggling to keep up with lead volume. We realized that artificial intelligence could solve this problem entirely. Our founding team combined decades of experience in machine learning, CRM systems, and local business operations to create a platform that doesn't just generate leads—it transforms how businesses grow. Today, we serve hundreds of local businesses across multiple industries, from real estate and fitness to home services and professional consulting.

Our mission is to democratize access to enterprise-grade AI technology for local businesses of all sizes. We believe that every business owner deserves the same sophisticated lead generation and automation tools that Fortune 500 companies use. By removing the complexity and cost barriers, we're empowering local entrepreneurs to compete on a level playing field. Our core values—transparency, reliability, and customer success—guide every decision we make. We're not just building software; we're building partnerships with our clients, committed to their long-term growth and success. Our vision is a future where every local business has access to AI-powered systems that handle the repetitive work, so business owners can focus on what they do best: building relationships and closing deals.
